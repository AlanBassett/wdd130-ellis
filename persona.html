What is this game?

Revelations: Persona (originally Megami Ibunroku Persona in Japan) is a spin-off of the Megami Tensei series, developed by Atlus. After the success of Shin Megami Tensei, Atlus wanted to reach a broader audience and experiment with new styles of storytelling. It was released in Japan in 1996 (PlayStation) and localized in North America in 1997
