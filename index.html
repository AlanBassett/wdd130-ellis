Digital Devil Story: Megami Tensei

Digital Devil Story: Megami Tensei was originally released in 1986 by Namco for the Famicom. It is based on a series of novels by Aya Nishitani.

Story Summary

The game follows a high school student, Akemi Nakajima, a genius programmer who creates a computer program capable of summoning demons. Alongside his classmate Yumiko Shirasagi, he becomes entangled in a supernatural conflict involving demons, otherworldly realms, and the struggle between order and chaos.

As demons invade reality, Nakajima must use his own summoning system to fight them, blurring the line between human control and demonic influence.
