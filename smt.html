Shin Megami Tensei

Shin Megami Tensei is the second game in the series. It is a dark, post-apocalyptic Japanese RPG released in 1992 for the Super Famicom by Atlus.

It builds on the earlier Digital Devil Story games but expands them into a deeper, more philosophical experience.
